In the meantime, here’s a general overview of how the Mentor (eDriving-style) app works, which is what most people mean 👇
What the Mentor Driving App Is

The Mentor app is a driver safety and behavior monitoring app. It uses your smartphone’s sensors to evaluate how safely you drive and provides coaching to reduce risk.

It’s commonly used by:

Companies with fleet or business drivers
Insurance-linked driver safety programs
New or high-risk driver coaching programs
How It Works (Step by Step)
1. Trip Detection
The app automatically detects when you start driving (usually above a certain speed).
You don’t normally need to press “start.”
2. Data Collection (Using Phone Sensors)

While driving, the app uses:

GPS → speed, routes, trip duration
Accelerometer & gyroscope → braking, cornering, acceleration
Phone interaction data → phone handling and distraction
3. Behaviors It Monitors

Typical behaviors tracked include:

Harsh braking
Rapid acceleration
Sharp cornering
Speeding
Phone use while driving (screen interaction, movement)
Overall smoothness and consistency

⚠️ It does not record audio or video in most cases—only motion and usage data.

4. Scoring System

After each trip, you receive:

A trip score
A daily/weekly overall score
Feedback highlighting risky behaviors

Higher scores = safer driving.

5. Coaching & Feedback

The app provides:

Short coaching videos
Tips based on your specific driving patterns
Trend reports showing improvement or decline
6. Reporting (If Used by an Employer)

Depending on setup:

Employers may see scores and trends
They usually cannot see personal phone data
Visibility is focused on safety metrics, not surveillance
Common Concerns (Quick Answers)
Does it track me when I’m not driving?
→ No, only during detected trips.
Does it track personal messages or calls?
→ No content is recorded.
Does it drain battery?
→ Moderate usage; usually optimized in the background.
Can passengers be mistaken for drivers?
→ Some apps allow you to mark “passenger trip.”
